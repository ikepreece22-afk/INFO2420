About Us

Native Cooking of Utah is a website dedicated to sharing the rich culinary traditions of Utah. From hearty Dutch oven recipes to sweet raspberry desserts, we celebrate the flavors that make Utah cuisine unique.

Our Story

Our team is passionate about preserving and sharing traditional Utah cooking methods and recipes. We believe that food is an important part of culture and heritage, and we want to make these recipes accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We are committed to documenting and celebrating Utah's native cooking traditions. Whether you are a seasoned cook or a beginner, we hope our site inspires you to try new recipes and explore the diverse flavors of Utah's culinary heritage.

Feel free to explore our recipe tributes, take our survey to share your favorite dishes, and check out our curated list of favorite culinary links. We are always looking to grow our community of food lovers!
